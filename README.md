## Electronics Projects
- MacBookPro Display Connector Replacement
  
  `A friend that used to work at GeekSquad had given me a recycled MacBookPro with a broken display. Everything was working fine
except for this one key component. So, what did I do? I ordered the $20 replacement cable, and because I'm lazy and didn't want to
disassemble the whole thing, I just cut and spliced it. Three years later, and the display is still going strong!`

- iPad Charging Port Fix
  
  `Back when I had a cheap soldering iron and was just learning through-hole (let alone surface-mount) I tried to replace this component
and ended up making it worse by ripping off some of the solder pads. Years later, after improving my skills and getting my hands on a nicer iron, I was able
to undo my disgusting work and save the device.`
